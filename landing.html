How It Works

Intelligent Conversations, Not Scripted Responses

Powered by advanced AI that understands intent, context, and nuance. Not just keyword matching — real semantic understanding.

Semantic Understanding

Uses AI embeddings to understand what customers mean, not just what they say. "I want my money back" and "How do I get a refund?" are understood as the same intent.

Shared Memory

Customer starts on chat, continues on voice call, then WhatsApp — the AI remembers everything. No "can you repeat that?" ever again.

Lightning Fast

Voice responses start in under 500ms — faster than human reaction time. Customers won't notice they're talking to AI.

Under The Hood

Enterprise AI, Made Simple

We handle the complex AI infrastructure so you don't have to. Here's what powers your AI assistant.

Vector Embeddings

Every customer message is converted into a mathematical representation (embedding) using OpenAI's text-embedding-3-small model. This allows the AI to understand meaning, not just keywords.

// How it works:
"Where's my order?" → [0.23, -0.45, 0.78, ...]
"Order status pls" → [0.24, -0.44, 0.77, ...]
// Cosine similarity: 0.97 → Same intent!
